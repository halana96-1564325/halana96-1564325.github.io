Doggos, puppers, and pupperinos!

If the title of this page is unfamiliar to you, you may not be well versed in DoggoLingo or Doggo-speak.

Some say puppies are "cute" to us humans because of their baby like features such as their large eyes compared to the rest of their head.

The love of dogs goes all the way back to at least 15,000 years ago. We know this because scientists have found dogs buried along with humans by 14,000 years ago.
